About the Showcase

The Final Year Project Showcase is an annual event celebrating the culmination of our students' hard work and innovation. It provides a platform for them to present their final projects, spanning a wide range of disciplines from graphic design and animation to architecture and digital media. This year, we are proud to host both a digital exhibition for a global audience and a physical event for an immersive, in-person experience.

Visit Our Physical Exhibition

Experience the projects firsthand and meet the creators. Join us at our main campus to immerse yourself in a world of creativity and innovation.

  • Dates: 15-17 November 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Location: Main Campus Exhibition Hall
